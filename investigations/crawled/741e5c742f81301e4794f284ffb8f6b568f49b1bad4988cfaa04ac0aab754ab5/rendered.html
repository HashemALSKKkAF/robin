Frequently asked questions

  • Who is Media Player Codec Pack Plus best for?

    Plus is for advanced users, HTPC owners, and anyone who wants the fullest codec pack with a broader set of components than the Standard edition.

  • Should I choose Plus or Standard?

    Choose Plus when you know you want the bigger toolkit and extra components. If you mostly want smooth everyday playback on a normal PC, Standard is usually the better pick.

  • Does Plus support common playback formats like MKV, MP4 and HEVC too?

    Yes. Plus still covers mainstream playback formats, but it goes further for users who want more flexibility and a larger install.

  • Is Plus still free?

    Yes. Plus is a free download with no registration required.

  • Will Plus be too much for an older PC?

    Possibly. On older or lower-power systems, Lite may be the better fit if you want a smaller install footprint.
